State of the Art Security

Your data is yours at all times, and we want to keep it that way. At Nize Systems, we have a zero-trust policy with client data, that means no one can access your data except without being authorized.

  • TLS/SSL Encryption
    Your data is always encrypted using the TLS protocol. TLS provides strong authentication, message privacy, and integrity interoperability, algorithm flexibility, and ease of deployment and use. Or as we like to say, it’s safe.
  • Key Based Authentication
    Our system has been taught to only allow access if they have the correct key. Because of the complexity of our security algorithms, breaking through this layer of security would take well over a lifetime, but we still rotate our keys frequently to ensure maximum security.
  • Onion Layer Security
    In addition to Key Based authentication, Nize Education Suite practices all standard security measures and has a variety of other proprietary security measures in place to detect and prevent possible security threats, so any attacker would be bringing a knife to this gunfight.
