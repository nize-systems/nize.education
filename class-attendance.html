With Presence, the teacher need not worry about entering attendance every day. The

teacher can start class with confidence, as students sign in to class with a tap of their

card. This system will allow substitute teachers to begin class on their own without

having to send a paper slip to the office during class. It also monitors students leaving

and entering class, such as for water or restroom breaks, eliminating the need for paper

sheets. The technology brings many more benefits to the table, such as live attendance

statistics and a simple dashboard to manage all the classes in one place.
