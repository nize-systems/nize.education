Nize Presence is a system designed to improve the efficiency of attendance, flex, and safety

features of a school. Currently, the act of taking attendance and keeping track of students is

an outdated, slow procedure. As such, through the use of the system, the arduous processes

will be streamlined and provide both administration and users with increases in efficiency,

information security, and student/staff safety.
