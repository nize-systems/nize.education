Nize Presence is a system designed to improve the efficiency of attendance, flex, and safety

features of a school. Currently, the act of taking attendance and keeping track of students is

an outdated, slow procedure. As such, through the use of the system, the arduous processes

will be streamlined and provide both administration and users with increases in efficiency,

information security, and student/staff safety.

 

What is Nize Presence?

Features

Class Attendance Automation

Student View

Teacher Dashboard

Presence Flex (Access)

 

Specifications

In our device, we have 3 main components: Raspberry Pi Zero W (microcontroller), RFID Reader RC522

(read and write RFID cards), touch screen (virtual keypad and display output). After extensive research and development, we have determined that these three components fit the best for its job for their costs. On the device, a Node.js app powers the touch screen relaying data back and forth to the server. On the server, we use standalone MongoDB instance to safely store the necessary student data. MongoDB comes with many built-in security protocols and is trusted by many leading organizations worldwide. In addition to MongoDB, we have built a custom API to validate the authenticity of users and provide only data as needed. All the dashboards and apps will be hosted on the server as well.

 

Security

The security of student data is our highest priority. At Nize, we follow a Zero-Trust policy meaning the server trusts nothing and nobody with any Personal Identifiable Information. With state-of-the art grade hashing and encryption techniques, we make sure every request is secure when in transit (eliminates Man in the Middle attacks) and while being processed.

 

Future Plans

In the future, we will focus on adding more features based on teacher/staff opinions and requests. Our current goals are to make the software more efficient and to reduce costs as much as possible. We are always constantly looking for vulnerabilities in our software and making sure that all personally identifiable information is kept secure. In general, we would like to continue to deliver a high-quality system that tackles several problems teachers face in the classroom everyday.
