About Us

WHAT WE DO

Dedicated to making education more efficient, safe, and personalized.

At every turn, Nize Systems strives to make education better for everyone involved. At every step of the process, from administrators, to teachers, to parents and students, Nize aims to better the processes that connects our educators to those that need them. Born in the heart of the silicon valley, the Nize team works tirelessly to ensure that we do our best for our clients.

  • Our Mission
  • Our Vision
  • Our Value

We strive to make education efficient, safe, and personalized to each individual student and teacher. Knowing that everyone's needs are different, the basis of our work revolves around the individual needs of everyone involved.

Through Presence, Presence Remote, and Nize Analytics, we envision a better education system. We know that through modern technology, we can cut the distance between educators and students, and vastly increase efficency.

At heart, we value the privacy, safety, and well-being of our educators, parents, and students. To us, each individual matters just as much as the whole, and we do our best to ensure that their needs are met.
