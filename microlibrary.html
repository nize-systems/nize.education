Nize MicroLibrary

Libraries. Redefined.



Microlibrary is built for teachers all around the world, whether you’re looking to make lending books easier or allow for easy collaboration between students; Microlibrary makes it easy to modernize your library. Microlibrary features beautiful hardware along with sophisticated software that pair to create the perfect library for your institution.






Features

  1. Easy Lending
    Our barcode-based checkout system makes lending quick and simple.
  2. Smart Book Recommendations
    Microlibrary learns each user's preferences and recommends books based on their history.
  1. Collaboration
    We support collaboration-focused reading groups for a more tight-knit classroom.
  2. Sophisticated Hardware
    Our hardware makes checking books in or out easier than ever.
